TSCM and Counter Surveillance

Technical Surveillance Counter Measures, or TSCM, is the process of securing a physical environment to compartmentalize all information withing that area, including spoken voice, visual information, and digital information. In addition to full RF, visual, thermal, and non-linear junction sweeps,  OBCYDIAN's TSCMs can be scoped to include forensic integrity analysis of all electronic devices within the defined area.
